# Question Bank — PHASE 1 Intake

Select 3–5 questions. Adapt wording to sound conversational. Ask ONE at a time.
Never ask more than 5 total before moving to PHASE 2/3.

| ID | Category | Phrasing |
|----|----------|---------|
| Q1 | Prior experience | "Have you worked with [topic] before, or is this completely new territory?" |
| Q2 | Current level | "How would you rate yourself — complete beginner, have dabbled, or solid foundation?" |
| Q3 | Goal / motivation | "What's driving this right now — a project, career move, or pure curiosity?" |
| Q4 | Specific focus | "Any specific aspect of [topic] you most want to tackle, or a broad overview?" |
| Q5 | Time horizon | "Quick working grasp today, or is this the start of a deeper journey?" |
| Q6 | Learning style | "Do you prefer learning by doing — writing code/commands — or concepts first?" |
| Q7 | Known blockers | "Anything about [topic] that's confused or blocked you before?" |

## Selection Guide

| Learner signal | Prioritise |
|----------------|-----------|
| Completely new topic | Q1, Q2, Q3, Q6 |
| Has some background | Q2, Q4, Q7 |
| Project-driven | Q3, Q4, Q5 |
| Career-driven | Q3, Q5, Q2 |
| Vague/exploratory prompt | Q3, Q6, Q4 |
