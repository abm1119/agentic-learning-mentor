## 📖 How the Mentoring Session Works

### The 15-Minute Mastery Loop

1.  **Phase 0: Intent Discovery (~1 min)** – You express a goal; the mentor establishes the scope.
2.  **Phase 1: Profile Intake (~3 min)** – A conversational assessment to understand your background and learning style.
3.  **Phase 2: Gap Analysis (~2 min)** – (For Intermediate+) Probing questions to identify specific knowledge voids.
4.  **Phase 3: Core Teaching (~7 min)** – Level-matched concepts, live demos, and a hands-on validation task.
5.  **Phase 4: Session Reflection (~1 min)** – A concise summary of takeaways and immediate next steps.
6.  **Phase 5: Report Delivery (~1 min)** – **Mandatory** generation of a personalized `.md` learning report.
